## 1. INVESTMENT

The Investor shall invest **CAD $10,000** (the "Investment") in exchange for **2% of the fully diluted equity** of the Company on a pre-money valuation of **CAD $500,000**.

## 2. SHARE TYPE

The Investment shall be issued as **Common Shares** with standard voting rights.

## 3. COMPANY CALL OPTION (BUYBACK)

### 3.1 Trigger
The Company shall have the right (but not the obligation) to repurchase all of the Investor's shares (the "Call Option") if the Company completes an equity financing of **CAD $100,000 or more** from third-party investors (a "Qualified Financing") within **24 months** of the date of this Term Sheet.

### 3.2 Buyback Price
The purchase price per share under the Call Option shall be **5× the original purchase price** (the "Buyback Price"), representing a total repurchase price of **CAD $50,000** for the Investor's full 2% stake.

### 3.3 Expiration
If no Qualified Financing occurs within 24 months, the Call Option shall expire, and the Investor shall retain their shares indefinitely, subject to standard transfer restrictions.

### 3.4 Mechanics
The Company may exercise the Call Option by providing written notice to the Investor within **30 days** of the closing of a Qualified Financing. Closing of the repurchase shall occur within **15 business days** of exercise.

## 4. USE OF FUNDS

The Investment shall be used for:
- Incorporation and legal fees
- Trademark filing and IP protection
- Hardware prototype development (LegiView v0)
- Software hosting and development tools
- Operating expenses and insurance

## 5. REPRESENTATIONS

### 5.1 Founder
The Founder represents that the Company is duly incorporated (or will be prior to closing), that the Founder is the sole owner of all intellectual property assigned to the Company, and that no other equity, options, or encumbrances exist.

### 5.2 Investor
The Investor represents that this investment is speculative, that the Investor can afford to lose the entire Investment, and that the Investor is not relying on any representation other than those contained herein.

## 6. GOVERNANCE

Until a Qualified Financing, the Founder shall retain sole control of the Company. The Investor shall not have a board seat or veto rights over ordinary business decisions.

## 7. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

The Investor may not transfer, sell, or pledge their shares without the prior written consent of the Founder, except to immediate family members or upon a change of control of the Company.

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

This Term Sheet and its contents shall be treated as confidential by both parties.

## 9. EXPENSES

Each party shall bear its own legal and advisory fees.

## 10. NON-BINDING

This Term Sheet is intended solely as a basis for further discussion and is **not legally binding** except for Sections 8 (Confidentiality) and 9 (Expenses), which shall be binding. The parties intend to enter into definitive agreements (Share Purchase Agreement, Shareholders Agreement) prior to closing.
