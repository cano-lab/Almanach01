Almanach

An AI companion for every student

Almanach is not a tutor that watches over your shoulder. It is a patient companion that walks beside you — noticing when you are stuck, celebrating when you figure it out, and gently nudging you back when you wander too far from the path you chose.

The Philosophy

"The AI is a companion, not a prison guard. The teacher is a guide, not a warden."

We believe students are allowed to wander. A child who starts coding a small game during a math lesson is not "off-topic" — they are engaged. The role of Almanach is to notice, to celebrate, and to offer a gentle path back, never a forced march.

Teachers receive insights, not alerts. There are no orange warning banners, no "off-topic" labels, no panic signals. Just soft, actionable notes that help teachers understand their students better.

How It Works

1

Student Opens Almanach

They see their current lesson, their progress, and a friendly chat interface. They can ask questions, work through problems, or explore related topics.

2

AI Companion Engages

Almanach responds with patience. It tracks understanding without testing. It notices confusion before the student asks for help. It celebrates breakthroughs quietly.

3

Teacher Receives Insights

A simple dashboard shows where the class is thriving, where individuals are struggling, and who might need a gentle check-in. No alerts. Just awareness.

4

Learning Continues

The student returns tomorrow to a companion that remembers yesterday's conversation, their sticking points, and their small victories. Continuity builds trust.
